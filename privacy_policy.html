Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Simple Countdown App ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our iOS mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection

Our App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All countdown data and settings are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Storage

  • All countdown information is stored exclusively on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user analytics are collected
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No third-party services are implemented

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

International Users

Our App is available worldwide and operates with the same no-data-collection policy regardless of your location. Since we don't collect any data, there are no international data transfers to consider.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Privacy Rights

Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83-1798.84, California residents are entitled to ask us for a notice identifying the categories of personal information which we share with our affiliates and/or third parties for marketing purposes, and providing contact information for such affiliates and/or third parties. Since we do not collect any user data, we have no information to share with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), since our App does not collect or process any personal data, we do not require a legal basis for data processing under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
